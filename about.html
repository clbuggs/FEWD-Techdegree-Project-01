About Me

I currently live in my hometown of Janesville, Wisconsin. I've been married since 2001 and have 2 children .

I work in the Technical Services department at the Wisconsin Center for the Blind and Visually Impaired, a state agency that serves children ages 3 through 21 who are blind or visually impaired as part of the Wisconsin Department of Public Instruction.

I have been visually impaired since birth. I have good usable sight in one eye that has been stable for many years. I use assistive technology to use computers and other devices.

Since I don't drive, I get around by public transit, rideshare services, and an occasional ride from family or friends. In general, I am very active and independent and view my disability as little more than an inconvenience.
